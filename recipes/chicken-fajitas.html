Description

A combination of chicken and bell peppers along with a ton of lime juice to make it extra juicy

Ingredients

  • Fajuta seasoning
  • Chicken
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with the fajita seasoning
  2. Cook the chiken on a large skillet with olive oil for about 7-8 minutes on each side
  3. While the chicken is cooking, cut the bell peppers and onions into thin strips
  4. Remove chicken and cut into strips and cook the peppers and onions in the same skillet until caramelized.
  5. Mix chicken back into skillet and add lime juice
