Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is a healthy Peruvian dish that is usually served as an appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Lemons
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Red onions
  • Jalapenos
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Chop all ingredients such as the shrimp, tomatoes, cilantro, etc.
  2. Mix together in a medium bowl
  3. Season with salt and pepper and lemon juice
  4. Allow food to marinate for at least 1 hour
  5. Serve over tortilla chips or tostadas
