Beef Taco Skillet

Description

A simple recipe I found online, it's essentially a healthy beef nacho dish

Ingredients

  • Taco seasoning
  • Ground beef
  • Black beans
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Tortilla chips
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook beef until browned, drain any excees fat if using a fattier beef, or add oil if using a leaner beef
  2. Rinse the black beans and add to the skillet, along with the taco seasoning.
  3. Stir together and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add tomatoes and continue to cook for about 3 minutes
  5. Add tortilla chips and mix around
  6. Add shredded cheese on top, cover and let sit for 2 minutes
